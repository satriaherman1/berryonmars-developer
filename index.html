What type of Developers can sign up?

We look for Developers with experience working as Fullstack, Backend, Frontend, DevOps, Data Analyst, Machine Learning, Architect, Team Lead, Product Manager or Product Owner roles. We look for talent from more than100 skills with the most popular being Angular, JavaScript, React, .NET, PHP, Phython, Swift and many more


Do you hire for freelance or permanent roles? What about part-time gigs?

We hire for both permanent and freelance roles. You set your preferences in your profile so we know what you're looking for. We don't offer part-time gigs as we focus on longer-term projects.


What makes BerryOnMars different from other remote-based job platforms?

We see ourselves as an automated Talent Agent for each individual Developer. We therefore want you to land not only great jobs but also get longer-term engagements, attractive pay and career growth opportunities as part of our community.

If you are looking for short-term or part-time gigs where you are one of a million offering the same services, there are other platforms that can help you.


How much do Berry Developers get paid and do I need to pay BerryOnMars any fees?

You set your own rates but if you need help then we are happy to advise you. With our experience, we will assess your value based on in-demand skills, you backgroun etc.

You do not pay any fees to BerryOnMars. As a Developer, signing up and getting matched to companies is completely free.


What kind of companies work with BerryOnMars and where are they located?

From early stage startups to large-scale multionationals, a host of companies work with BerryOnMars. The majority of our customers are based in the US and Europe.
